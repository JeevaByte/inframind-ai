# Roadmap Validation

> [CHANGELOG](../../CHANGELOG.md). The strategic analysis below remains valid;

## Purpose

This document validates the original product roadmap against current market
conditions, technical feasibility, and team capacity.

## Original Roadmap Summary

1. **Phase 1 — Core linting engine** (complete)
2. **Phase 2 — AI-powered rule suggestions** (in progress)
3. **Phase 3 — Policy-as-code integrations** (planned)
4. **Phase 4 — Enterprise dashboards and reporting** (planned)

## Validation Results

| Phase | Feasibility | Market Fit | Priority |
|-------|------------|------------|----------|
| 1 | ✅ Complete | ✅ Confirmed | Shipped |
| 2 | ✅ High | ✅ High | Current |
| 3 | ✅ High | ✅ High | Next |
| 4 | ⚠️ Medium | ✅ High | Future |

## Adjustments

- Phase 2 scope trimmed to focus on top 5 rule categories by user demand.
- Phase 4 moved behind Phase 3 based on customer feedback prioritisation.

## Next Review

Schedule roadmap validation quarterly or after major customer interviews.
